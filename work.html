Workout of the Day Vol.2

Warm-up

Spider Lunge and Rotation

Perform 10 repetitions alternating sides.

Rocking Lunges

Perform 5-8 reps per leg, hold for a moment to stretch.

Workout

AMRAP for 20 minutes:

  • 5 Dumbbell Burpee to Press
  • 10 Push-ups
  • 15 Dumbbell Reverse Lunges
  • 20 V-Up
  • 30 Double Unders/Claps or 50 Jumping Rope

Cool-down

Stretch for 20-30 seconds for each muscle group.
